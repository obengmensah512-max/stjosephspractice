Our Story

St. Joseph's Practice Primary School was founded in 1985 by the Catholic Diocese. What began as two classrooms under a mango tree has grown into a modern school with 22 classrooms, science lab, computer center, and playground.

📌 Our Mission

To nurture confident, compassionate learners who excel academically and live their values.

👁️ Our Vision

To be a beacon of holistic, affordable, and excellent primary education.
